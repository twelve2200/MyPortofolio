Strategic Tick-Tack-Toe (temporary name)

Strategic Tick-Tack-Toe is a 1v1 strategy game with a twist on traditional tic-tac-toe. Each player controls three pawns that can be moved according to the field's path. The objective is to align three pawns horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win. The game offers three different fields and two modes: online and offline.

Status : in Development

Ethan the Hero (temporary name)

Ethan the Hero is a 2D pixel art side scroller with focus on stories and action. The main mechanic about this game is the main character got traits that if he didnt kill monster whitin certain time he will die. Here is the synopsis story.

In a world where magic, monsters, demons, and gods coexist, an untalented aristocratic boy struggles with both magic and swordsmanship. Burdened by high expectations from his parents and society as the family's successor, he grows up enduring harsh bullying from his siblings and scorn from those around him. This relentless mistreatment fosters a delinquent personality and a life of trouble, ultimately leading to his aristocratic title being stripped away and his expulsion from home.

Lost and destitute, he finds himself at a church, drunk and unconscious. In a twist of fate, he accidentally breaks an ancient sword artifact and is impaled by it. As he teeters on the brink of death, a mysterious female voice offers him a chance to live. With a faint, hopeless "yes," he learns that the voice belongs to the church’s goddess. She imposes a dire ultimatum: if he fails to kill a demon or monster within certain time, his heart will stop. "If you want to live," she commands, "you must struggle, defeat all the demons and monsters on the surface, and close the dungeons on Earth. Only then will I set you free."

Meanwhile, on another continent, thousands of dungeons suddenly appear, unleashing hordes of monsters and demons upon the world. An all-out war erupts between humanity and the demon-controlled beasts. The demon realm, suffering from barren soil and unending conflict, has begun its invasion of the human world. In response, the gods intervene, selecting champions to act as intermediaries and granting them new powers.

This story is not about a hero celebrated for saving the world, but rather about a boy’s desperate struggle for freedom in a world where his very survival hangs in the balance.
